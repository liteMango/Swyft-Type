About Us

Welcome to Swyft Type! Our platform was created by a passionate team of developers and designers who came together with a shared goal: to create an intuitive, engaging, and effective speed typing game. We wanted a tool that would not only be fun but also help users improve their typing speed and accuracy.

The Swyft Type team consists of dedicated individuals from different backgrounds, including software development, UX/UI design, and educational technology. By combining our expertise, we designed a platform that is easy to use, visually appealing, and challenging enough to help you level up your typing skills.

Our mission is simple: empower people of all ages to become faster and more accurate typists. We believe that typing is a fundamental skill in today's digital world, and practicing through a dynamic game can make learning more enjoyable.
